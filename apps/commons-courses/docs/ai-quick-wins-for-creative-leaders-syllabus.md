## Learning Experience

This course is built around one principle: **you learn by building, not by watching.**

Every lesson is structured as a walkthrough — you follow along in your own tools, building the actual workflow as the lesson runs. By the time the lesson ends, you have something working.

**Watch** — A focused 15–25 minute walkthrough. No fluff, no preamble. We show you the tool, show you the workflow, and show you the result.

**Build** — You follow along in your own account. Every lesson has a "build this now" moment where you replicate the workflow for your context.

**Keep** — Every workflow, template, and prompt you build during the course is yours. The course is the work.

**Ship** — Each module ends with one thing switched on in your real operation — not saved for later, not left as an exercise.

## Lesson Plans

### Module 1 — Your First AI Wins — This Week

*Skip the theory. Get three AI workflows running before the week is out using tools you already have access to.*

**4 LESSONS**

**1. The 20-Minute AI Audit** *(15 min — Free Preview)*
Before adding anything new, find out what you're already paying for that has AI built in. A fast audit of your existing stack — Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Notion, Canva — and where the quick wins are hiding right now.

**2. Turn Your Inbox into an Assistant** *(18 min — Free Preview)*
Use AI inside Gmail or Outlook to draft replies, summarise long threads, and triage briefs and requests. A practical setup walkthrough you can complete during the lesson — by the end, your inbox works differently.

**3. AI for Briefs — From Rough Notes to Ready-to-Share** *(20 min)*
Turn a rough voice note, bullet list, or messy email chain into a clean, structured creative brief in under five minutes. ChatGPT and Claude prompts that actually work for creative contexts — not generic templates.

**4. Meeting Notes to Action List — Automatically** *(15 min)*
Record a meeting, get a summary, extract action items, and push them to your project tool — without touching a keyboard. A full walkthrough using tools available at every budget level, from free to enterprise.

**MODULE 1 ASSIGNMENT**
*First Win Live* — Complete the 20-minute audit of your existing stack. Identify your single highest-leverage quick win and get it switched on before the week ends. Screenshot it and share it in the community.

---

### Module 2 — Connect Your Tools — No Developer Needed

*Wire together the tools your team already uses — Google Forms, Sheets, WhatsApp, email, Slack — with AI in the middle. The automations creative teams actually need.*

**4 LESSONS**

**1. How Tool Connections Work — The Non-Technical Version** *(15 min)*
Triggers, actions, and data — the three concepts behind every automation. A visual explanation of how tools talk to each other and where AI fits in the chain. No jargon, no code.

**2. Google Forms → Sheets → WhatsApp Notifications** *(25 min)*
The workflow creative leaders keep asking for. A step-by-step build: collect a form response, log it to Sheets, and fire a WhatsApp message to the form owner and the respondent — automatically. Built with n8n, no code required. You will have this running by the end of the lesson.

**3. Automated Client Intake and Brief Confirmation** *(22 min)*
A client fills in a form, AI summarises their brief, sends them a confirmation with a cleaned-up version of what they submitted, and pings your team in Slack. The full flow, built live. A workflow your clients will notice.

**4. Building Your Own Workflow — A Template to Steal** *(20 min)*
A reusable n8n template for creative intake, approval routing, and delivery confirmation. How to adapt it to your specific tools, team size, and client process — and what to watch out for when things break.

**MODULE 2 ASSIGNMENT**
*Workflow Live* — Build the Google Forms → Sheets → WhatsApp notification workflow in your own n8n account. Test it with a real form submission and confirm the notification fires correctly.

---

### Module 3 — Work Leaner with AI

*Identify where your creative operation is carrying unnecessary weight — and use AI to stretch your team further without adding headcount or budget.*

**4 LESSONS**

**1. Where Creative Teams Carry the Most Unnecessary Weight** *(18 min)*
A clear-eyed look at the five heaviest activities in a typical creative operation — and which of them AI can lighten materially today, not in theory. A framework for prioritising where to start.

**2. AI for Content Production — Stretch Your Existing Team Further** *(22 min)*
Repurpose one piece of content into ten using AI. Blog to LinkedIn to email to social captions to short video script — a practical workflow that multiplies your team's output without adding headcount or budget.

**3. Maximise What You're Already Paying For** *(18 min)*
An audit of common creative tool subscriptions — stock imagery, copywriting tools, transcription services, translation — and the AI capabilities already included in tools you are paying for but likely underusing.

**4. Building a Simple Efficiency Dashboard** *(15 min)*
Track the time your AI workflows are freeing up so you can make the case to leadership — and know where to keep investing. A Google Sheets template you can set up in 20 minutes and keep running indefinitely.

**MODULE 3 ASSIGNMENT**
*Efficiency Audit* — Complete the tool subscription audit from Lesson 3. Identify at least two tools you can get more from with AI. Set up the efficiency dashboard and log your first week of gains.

---

### Module 4 — Grow Your Offer with AI

*Turn the capabilities your team is building into new opportunities — new services, faster delivery, and offers you couldn't have made before.*

**4 LESSONS**

**1. New Services You Can Offer Because of AI** *(20 min)*
AI lowers the cost of production fast enough that services that were previously out of reach — rapid concept sprints, localisation, personalised creative at scale, always-on social content — are now viable. A practical scan of what to add to your offer today.

**2. Productising Your Creative Process** *(22 min)*
Turn a bespoke creative service into a structured product with a fixed scope, fixed price, and AI-assisted delivery. How to package it, price it, and pitch it to clients who want speed and certainty over open-ended custom work.

**3. Faster Delivery as a Competitive Advantage** *(18 min)*
When your team can turn a brief around in 48 hours instead of two weeks, that is a genuine differentiator. How to position speed — and what it takes operationally to deliver on that promise consistently without burning your team out.

**4. Your 30-Day AI Growth Plan** *(20 min)*
Capstone lesson: identify one new opportunity, build one workflow that enables it, and set a 30-day target for what it should unlock. A simple one-page plan you can act on the day you finish this course.

**MODULE 4 ASSIGNMENT — FINAL PROJECT**
*30-Day Growth Plan* — Write your one-page plan: one new offer or capability, one workflow that enables it, and a 30-day target. Share it in the community and get feedback from peers doing the same.
